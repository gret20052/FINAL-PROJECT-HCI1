Academic Programs

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics

Discover the world through scientific inquiry, technological innovation, and mathematical precision. Our STEM strand prepares future engineers, scientists, doctors, and technology innovators with rigorous curriculum and hands-on experiences that foster critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

ABM

Accountancy, Business & Management

Develop your business acumen and financial literacy in our ABM strand. Future entrepreneurs, accountants, marketing professionals, and business leaders will thrive in our comprehensive program that combines theory with practical applications in today's dynamic economic landscape.

GAS

General Academic Strand

Explore diverse academic disciplines and keep your career options open with our flexible GAS strand. This well-rounded program provides strong foundations across multiple subjects, preparing students for various college paths while developing versatile skills applicable to numerous career fields.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences

The HUMSS strand is designed for students who are passionate about human behavior, social issues, and communication. Primary School

Building Strong Foundations

Our Primary School program lays the essential groundwork in literacy, numeracy, science, and the arts. It focuses on developing curiosity, creativity, and strong values, helping young learners grow into confident and responsible individuals prepared for their academic journey ahead.

Junior High School

A Time for Discovery and Growth

Our Junior High School program bridges the gap between primary education and senior high. It offers a well-rounded curriculum designed to enhance academic skills, character development, and personal responsibility. Students explore various subjects to help them identify their interests and potential future paths while preparing for more specialized senior high programs.
