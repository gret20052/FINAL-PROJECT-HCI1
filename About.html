About PASDI

Discover our journey of academic excellence and Buddhist values

Philippine Academy of Sakya Davao Inc. (PASDI) was established in 1995 as an extension of the Philippine Academy of Sakya Manila, a Chinese-Filipino educational institution rooted in the teachings of Sakyamuni Buddha.

The Davao branch was founded to provide quality education that integrates academic excellence, Filipino-Chinese heritage, and Buddhist values in Southern Mindanao.

Starting with a small number of enrollees, PASDI gradually expanded its curriculum and facilities over the years, eventually becoming one of the respected private schools in Davao City.

PASDI serves students from Preschool to Senior High School, offering strands such as STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) and ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management).

The school emphasizes holistic development, encouraging students to grow not just intellectually, but also emotionally, socially, morally, and spiritually. Buddhist values such as compassion, discipline, humility, and wisdom are central to the school's philosophy.
